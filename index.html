Towards the Persuasiveness of Smart Voice Assistants

We are studying how user trust can be improved in Smart Voice Assistants (SVA) in their online shopping decisions. Through an interactive study, we capture users' trust in synthesized voices with varying voice characteristics.

Methods: Voice Synthesis, Quantitative analysis, Interactive user study design

Tools: Python, R, Microsoft Speech Studio, Qualtrics

Past Project

Decaying Photos for Enhanced Privacy

We propose two temporal redaction methods while posting photos on social media. These methods involve sensitive contents of a posted photo being decayed gradually so that the contents are not identifiable after a certain period. We quantitatively compare user behavior towards the proposed methods with existing sharing mechanisms and found 17-21% participants prefer the proposed methods when the photos do not contain any identifiable information or the photos are shared with close contact.

Methods: Quantitative analysis, User study design

Tools: R, Qualtrics

Publication: CSCW'22
